Just a moment

By Louise H.

Jan 5, 2019


I sit here on the bed, my friend on my left.


We wrestle with buttons. On the controllers we pressed. 


Leaning left.
                        leaning right.
Yelling at the bright screen.


It's one of the few things, I've missed since nineteen.


I've fussed, and stressed. Over family, and life,


But now I yell at a the tv. To a fictional character's strife.


It's the little things I tend to enjoy. Things, I knew, I had to ignore.


But now, for just a moment, I get to enjoy it once more.


Sitting forward on the couch, ready to settle the score.
