The Farmhouse

I glanced between the house and my friends. This whole thing felt uncomfortable to me.

With a heavy sigh, and wheeled my bike back a little, and looked down.

"I-I'm sorry, but I..I don't like this"

Steph looked a little upset, but gave a gentle smile.

"..hey..it's okay. If you want, you can wait here. We'll be in and out in a second, okay?"

I nodded, but there was a tinge of guilt building.


"Alright ladies, let's go." Steph started to pedal her bike towards the farmhouse.

Niyanna sighed and followed behind.

Chelsea patted my shoulder. "We'll be back before you know it."


I watched them ride up to that old farmhouse. I watched them leave their bike outside.


I waited....


and waited...


But they never came out.


I remember debating for so long that my stomach churned. My friends were gone for so long.

Eventually I ran for another house, and begged to use their phone. I called for help.

When the police arrived, they kept asking all these questions. And when they searched...


...They found nothing



BAD ENDING
