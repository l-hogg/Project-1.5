The Farmhouse

September, 1988.


Fall was upon us. The warm air swept away by the cold front rolling in.

Times for shorts, and tank tops were over.

The time for sweaters, pumpkins spice, and falling leaves had begun.

I rode with my friends; Chelsea, Niyanna, and Steph. We passed some farm houses.

We passed them everyday. Without a single thought.

Until there was an incident at the final house sitting at the end of the road.

This home belonged to an old couple, or at least that's what we were told.

No one has ever seen their faces, or are even sure they exist.

But something happened there a few days ago. This quiet house was surrounded by sirens.


Rumours spread in our little town of what happened.

Some say there was a murder, others assume some ritural thing. Maybe a cult?

No matter what people think, there hasn't been a report submitted yet.

Chelsea believes the Police are trying to hide the truth.

Niyanna thinks it was just a dumb prank.

But Steph assumes it was something unnatural. Or supernatural.

I'm not a fan of all those stories. They go to dark places, and sometimes you can't get out.

Or maybe I was just being paranoid.


I looked up from my handlebars and pulled the breaks hard, skidding across the gravel to a stop.

My friends were staring at the final house on the road.
