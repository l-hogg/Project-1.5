The Farmhouse


Steph was in awe, leaning over her handlebars.

"There is it. The murder house"

Niyanna rolled her eyes and sat back on her bike.

"Steph, it's just an old house. Nothing happened there"

Steph gave a dramatic, and sarcastic gasp.

"Oh I'm sorry. Did you not see the lights surrounding this place?"

Her friend gave a heavy sigh

"I know-I know, but there has been no report since, right? So maybe-"

She was cut off by Chelsea who shrugged dramatically.

"What it was nothing? No, no. Something happened here. They just don't wanna say"

I glanced to the house. It stood in that very spot for decades. Some say it was built when the town was founded.

It loomed in the distance. Its white paint job had chipped away with age. Roof slats were missing. Dust covered the windows.

It doesn't seem like anyone ever lived in this house.


I was snapped out of my thoughts when Steph asked me a question. "What do you think?"

I looked up to see three pairs of eyes watching me.

Maybe it was deer in the headlights looke, or maybe they just knew me too well, but they smiled gently.

"Off in thought again, Mel?"

My face began to turn pink in embarassment, but I quickly tried to play it off.

"No..no..I-I heard you. I was just-"

"Thinking?" Chelsea grinned.

"...yeah.." I chuckled nervously.

Steph wheeled her bike over to me.

"Well now that you're back, I was asking you guys what you think"

"About what?"

"About going inside"
