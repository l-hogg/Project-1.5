The Farmhouse


I was in shock. Was Steph serious? Sure it was an empty house, but it belongs to someone, right?

Steph tried to sound convincing, "Oh come on. It will be so cool to check out"

Chelsea glanced to the Niyanna and me, "Steph, I love your enthusiasm, but we can't just go in there"

"Oh really? Miss "Nobody can hide the truth from me" is gonna play safe? Come on, I know you wanna look

Chelsea raised her hands in protest, "I know, I know, but it could be dangerous."

"That's the whole point of an adventure, Chelsea. To see what happened, and find the truth!"

Steph put her hands on her hips, and pushed her chest out to pose like Superman. Chelsea couldn't help but laugh.

See, Chelsea loves to know the truth. Sometimes Steph would call her 'Lois' like Superman's girlfriend.

She was always determined to solve the unsolvable if she could. So this must have been itching at her.


Niyanna shrugged in response, "Know what? Screw it. I'm in, only to prove it's just an old house."

Steph pumped her arms in success, and then entended a hand out to me.

"You coming?"
